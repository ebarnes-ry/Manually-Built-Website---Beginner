for the DC artist

art

We cover the scope of art and where to find artistic scenes within the DC/Maryland/Virginia area. All the galleries, exhibitions, independent artists, and your own works that you submit to us are topics of interest here!

music

Music scenes in the area are one of the best ways to connect to other people in person and online. We not onyl provide reccomendations to our favorite local and global bands, but where to find live shows in the area and when. This ranges from ticket updates, to small bands and big bands alike. Message us to have your event/show showcased!

food

This is the best place for reccomendations of any range of food in the area. Between the cheapest quickest eats, to where to find your favourite foods, to the best high end eats worth your money - we've got it! Contact us to register your business/grandmas kitchen to get an article showcase!

our mission

We aim to connect creative individuals within the DC, Maryland, Virginia area to creative scenes for inspiration. We understand it's difficult to break into the right communities of people in a city such as this one, which is why we created this resource for connection, inspiration, and to facilitate your next adventure. Feel free to contact us with your reccomendations, your music, restaurant, or general questions you may have at any time! -Love, the team here :)
